XML Data Dumps

For hackers and legal analysts, we provide XML dumps of our data. We generate these files on the first of the month, when we create individual dump files for each court, and a single file containing our entire corpus (latest-all.xml.gz). These dumps contain our entire corpus, and can thus be very large.

The information in the dumps should be self explanitory, and can be discussed in our forum.

All time stamps in these documents are Pacific Standard Time.
