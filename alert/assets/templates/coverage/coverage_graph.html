General Notes

Charts indicate the number of cases that we have in our database for a given year. These numbers include both precedential and non-precedential opinions for any given court.

We have sourced our data from Resource.org, a donation from Lawbox LLC. and directly from the court websites, with the following notes:

  • Our coverage includes the majority of the Supreme Court record. We sourced this from Resource.org and Lawbox LLC., which most people consider complete for 1754 to 2004. Unfortunately, we have identified some small gaps, especially around the very beginning of this corpus.
  • F.3d and F.2d from Resource.org and Lawbox LLC. are included with no known gaps for for the years 1950-2007.
  • Starting in 2010 we have scraped opinions from many Federal Court websites.
  • We have scraped the historical content off many court websites, merging it with F3 where appropriate.

We believe that our coverage of the Supreme Court and F3 is the best available on the open Web, but we admit we have some gaps in several of our other courts. If you have questions or would like more detail, please let us know.

For more information about our jurisdictions, see our jurisdiction page.
